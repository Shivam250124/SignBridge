How to Use SignBridge

For Deaf Users (Left Panel)

  • Position your hands in front of the camera
  • Make ISL gestures — they'll be recognised automatically
  • Your gestures are converted to text and spoken aloud
  • Toggle TTS on/off as needed

For Hearing Users (Right Panel)

  • Click "Start Speaking" and speak clearly
  • Or type your message and press Send
  • The matching sign language video plays immediately below

Tips

  • Use Chrome or Edge for speech recognition
  • Ensure good lighting for the camera
  • Keep hands fully visible in the camera frame
